Juan David Hernandez Leal - Economics PhD Researcher

Hi, I am Juan David Hernandez Leal, an Economics PhD researcher at the University of California, San Diego.

My research studies how financial institutions, technology, and policy shape credit access, repayment, and borrower outcomes. I am especially interested in financial inclusion, debt repayment, borrower communication, and the role of AI in financial services.

Current projects use operational data, surveys, and field implementation to understand how borrowers interact with repayment systems and how institutions can design outreach that is effective, transparent, and respectful.

Research

My current work focuses on AI-assisted repayment conversations, debt recovery and mediation, and financial inclusion datasets that connect CRM systems, call records, payment plans, surveys, and repayment outcomes.

  • Financial inclusion
  • Credit access and repayment
  • Debt collection and mediation
  • AI and automation in financial services
  • Field experiments and administrative data

Teaching

I teach and support courses in economics, data analysis, and applied research methods.
